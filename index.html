About

We are manufacturer of nickel, cadmium and leaad related Industrial grade chemicals. started by Dr. Ratan Prakash Jain in 1988., we are commited to deliver excellence in all our products.

Dr. Jain & Rishabh Chemical Company

Well equipped with state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and technological advancements, we, Dr. Jain & Rishabh Chemical Company, are counted as one of the most reputed entities in the domain of industrial chemicals. we embarked our journey, with a vision to mark our prominence in the worldwide market as well as to attain a leading position. with an uncompromising commitment towards our clients, we have successfully carved a niche in the industry. today, we are known as a renowned manufacturer and supplier of a qualitative assortment of Chemicals. all our chemicals are developed under the supervision of qualified research analysts and quality controllers.

Understanding the importance of safety in our domain, we take every possible precautionary measure while manufacturing our range. moreover, our production staff strictly adheres to superior industrial as well as safety standards. we also lay strong emphasize on continual improvisation of our chemicals, and thus carry out rigorous market research from time to time. this helps us in upgrading our product assortment in terms of composition, usage as well as quality. in addition, we employ latest and innovative techniques of production so as to keep pace with the changing market requirements.

Our Products

Nickel Carbonate

Known for its high effectiveness, the offered Nickel Carbonate is one of the finest that is available in the market. The manufacture of this chemical involves the use of high grade ingredients.

nickel ammonium sulphate

NiSO4·(NH4)2SO4·6H2O A green, crystalline compound, soluble in water; used as a nickel electrolyte for electroplating. Also known as ammonium nickel sulfate; double nickel salt.

Nickel Acetate

We are engaged in offering genuine quality Nickel Acetate to the customers. Nickel acetate is widely being used in electroless plating and is also considered suitable for the production and use of sealing agents for oxidised aluminum.

lead sulphate

Our expert team members are engaged in manufacturing these products by implementing latest technologies. Our lead sulphate are usually used in Paint, Rubber & Battery Industries.

cadmium carbonate

These are used for manufacturing polyester, glass pigment flux, organic catalysts, plastic plasticizer, stabilizer and raw material for production of cadmium salt. These are offering at market leading prices.

cadmium oxide

The offered cadmium oxide is used in cadmium plating baths, electrodes for storage batteries, cadmium salts, catalyst, ceramic glazes, phosphors, and nematocide. This cadmium oxide can be found as a colorless amorphous powder or as brown or red crystals.
